# Openness

GrowlerDB is **open end to end**: **Apache-2.0** licensed, with both the search engine and the index
store open — not a closed appliance. This is a non-negotiable design constraint, not just a
distribution choice.

**Status.** Met; the [git repository](/product/interfaces/git-repo.md) is the open-source home.
